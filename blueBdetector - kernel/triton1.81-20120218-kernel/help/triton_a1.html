Notes

  • 1 byte = 8 bits
  • 1 sector=512 bytes
  • 1 Rawfile = 60,000 sectors
  • for additional information see comments in: read_rawHARPdata.m, read_rawHARPdir.m, read_rawHARPhead.m, and hrp2xwav.m
  • HARP Data are recorded with Motorola CPU (little Endian) and some 2 byte, 4 byte words need to be swapped at byte level
